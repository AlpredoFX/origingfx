# OriginGFX

**Gallery First. Store Second. Artist Always.**

OriginGFX is a modern digital gallery built for Minecraft artists to showcase their work, grow their portfolios, and connect with a wider audience. Instead of focusing on selling first, OriginGFX puts artworks and the artists behind them at the center of the experience.

The project began as a personal portfolio created by **Alpredo FX**, with a simple goal: to build a place where digital artworks can be discovered beyond the limits of social media algorithms. Over time, that idea evolved into a platform where talented artists can present their creations, share their stories, and make it easier for people to discover their work.

## Features

* Modern gallery-style interface
* Artist portfolio pages
* Artwork detail pages
* Category-based exploration
* Responsive design
* Light & Dark mode
* SEO-friendly structure
* Fast and minimal user experience
* Community-focused artist profiles

## Project Vision

OriginGFX is designed to become more than just a portfolio website. The long-term vision is to create a digital home for Minecraft artists, where every artwork has its own space, every artist has a professional portfolio, and visitors can discover creators naturally before deciding to commission or support them.

Rather than functioning as a traditional marketplace, OriginGFX aims to bridge artists and their audience through a gallery-first experience.

## Current Status

OriginGFX is currently under active development. New features, UI improvements, and artist pages are continuously being added as the project grows.
